Who is

Abhinna Adhikari?

Abhinna is a Computer Science and Engineering honors student at UConn. He is currently a junior and will graduate in May 2021.

  • Origins
  • Personality
  • Interests

Abhinna was born in Nepal in 1999 and moved to America with his parents at the age of 4. He has since lived in Albany NY, Edinburg TX, Cambridge MA, and Natick MA. As his childhood was mostly spent in Cambridge, that is where he considers home.

Abhinna is an open minded person who doesn’t take himself too seriously. He enjoys interacting with people of different views and experiences because he likes learning about people that are different than him. He also enjoys problem solving and is very competitive so you’ll never see him back down from a challenge.

Abhinna considers himself a primarily backend developer, however; he isn’t certain about what he wants to focus on yet. He hopes as he gains more experience in the various concentrations, he will find his area. He is very versatile and can pick up concepts and tools quickly, so learning new things are never too much of a challenge. He is always excited to learn.

Abhinna's

Hobbies

One of Abhinna’s favorite hobbies is kayaking. He enjoys the feeling of the wind flowing through his hair while he gently traverses the waters, exploring new areas by kayak. When he is on the water, all his worries and stresses disappear for several hours. You can often find him kayaking in the Charles River during the summer.

Kayaking

Abhinna’s two favorite sports are soccer and basketball. He enjoys playing and watching both, however, he definitely prefers playing. He has always supported the soccer and basketball teams Manchester United FC and the Celtics respectively, since he was a child. His favorite soccer player is Marouane Fellaini and his favorite basketball player is James Harden.

Soccer and Basketball

Abhinna has recently picked up bouldering because of his university’s new climbing wall. Although it is new to him, he enjoys it very much and will continue to participate until he gets better. He hopes to transition to outdoor rock climbing once he becomes adept enough at bouldering.

Bouldering

Abhinna enjoys writing and painting. Although he doesn’t know about what exactly yet, he hopes to write a book one day. His favorite book is The Catcher in the Rye and his favorite painting is Melody of the Night by Leonid Afremov. The painting he is most proud of is one of handsome squidward.
